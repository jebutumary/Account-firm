Our Partners

ADESANYA , ADEYINKA MATTHEW, MSC, FCTI, FCA

A 1990 (EMA) graduate from University of Lagos and MSc Accounting from Leeds Metropolitan University, Leeds, UK. Mr. Adeyinka Adesanya joined Bisi Adegboye & Co as an Audit Senior and rose to the position of a Manager. He is a Fellow of both the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria and Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria.

He was involved in the audit of Federal Government Schools and also Federal Ministry of Education World Bank project as a staff of the Accountant General of the Federation. He was pioneer head of Finance and Admin in some Mortgage Institutions including Centage Savings & Loans Ltd, Milestone Savings & Loans Ltd and a former lecturer at Federal Polytechnic Ilaro

Mr. Adesanya, is currently the Managing Partner of Yinka Adesanya & Co (Chartered Accountants & Tax Consultants). He has varied work experience spanning over twenty years in the practice of Auditing, Accountancy, Taxation, Financial Advisory, Government Institutions, Industry and Commerce in Nigeria.

ADEDOKUN, SAMSON OLUWASEGUN FCA, ACFE, MBA

A 1989 graduate of Accounting from the Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro, and MBA from University of Calabar. He trained and qualified as a professional accountant with the firm of EY (Chartered Accountants). He is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria and Associate member of Institute of Fraud Examiners.

Samson has worked with various organizations and was involved in re- engineering and restructuring of many organizations while at EY. He has quality experience in managing audit team in order to maintain effective and risk focused audit function.

His experience cuts across Business Processes, Regional audits, Operations and Maintenance, Cash Management, Sales and Marketing, General Accounting, Warehouse Management and Controls and giving recommendations for process improvement and mitigation of risks. He also worked with both Airtel Nigeria and Globacom Ltd in various management capacities.

Mr. Adedokun brings to the firm 20 years of experience in Auditing, Accounting, Taxation, Human Resources and Financial Advisory Services.

ODIANA, KINGSLEY FCA, MBA

A graduate of Accounting from the Ambrose Ali University, Ekpoma, and MBA from Lagos State University. Kingsley is an Associate member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN), the Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria (CITN) and Chartered Institute for Securities and Investment (CISI) United Kingdom. He is also an alumnus of the New York Institute of Finance (USA) and the International Faculty of Finance (IFF, United Kingdom).

Prior to joining the firm, Mr Odiana worked as the Chief Finance Officer of BGL Group; Nigerian German Chemicals PLC as an Accountant with responsibilities for Credit Management; Food & Pharma Limited as Finance Manager. He was also with Olawale Coker & Co (a firm of Chartered Accountants). He has received specialized training in Corporate Treasury Management, Enterprise Risk Management, Tax Management, Asset and Liability Management and Audit Management.

Mr. Odiana brings to the firm 15 years of cognate experience in financial management, auditing, tax management, corporate treasury / risk management, accounting and financial reporting.

POPOOLA, KAYODE LANRE FCA, MBA, PGSEF

Kayode Popoola is a 1989 graduate of accounting from the Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro, MBA from the Esut Business School, postgrad diploma from Dublin City University in Sustainable Energy Finance and also a certificate in Financial Crime Prevention from the Professional School of Finance in Ireland.

He has worked in various organisations both locally and internationally, he has worked as the finance director of Nigerian German Chemical Plc, Chief Accountant for Consolidated Breweries Plc, Financial Accountant for XEROX HS Nigeria and at international level with Unilever as Financial Accountant, Pfizer as Project Accountant and at Ingersoll Rand as Senior Financial Compliance Analyst. He had his audit training at Femi Vigo & Co. and Adebola Oleolo & Co., both are firms of Chartered Accountants.

Mr. Popoola has over 20 years of experience in Auditing, Accounting, Statutory reporting , System implementation, Process Audit, Organisation Restructuring, Strategy formulation and implementation.
